Words of Advice

These are some tips I heard that helped me during the application process. Feel free to listen, or not. It's up to you.

1. Try to get fee waivers; all 30 of the schools I applied to were free.

2. Be niche

3. If you want to get into a top school try Early Decision.

4. Don't limit yourself. Apply to as many schools as you want. You miss 100% of the shots you don't take.

5. Have fun
